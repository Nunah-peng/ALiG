About Our Association

The Association of Liberians in Gisenyi (ALG) was established to create a sense of home and belonging for Liberians residing in the beautiful city of Gisenyi, Rwanda. We are a non-profit organization dedicated to fostering unity, cultural exchange, and mutual support among our members and the local Rwandan community.

Since our founding, we have been a hub for social gatherings, cultural celebrations, and community initiatives, strengthening the bonds of our diaspora.

Our Mission

Community Building

To foster a strong, united, and supportive community for all Liberians in Gisenyi.

Cultural Preservation

To celebrate and preserve the rich cultural heritage of Liberia through events and education.

Support & Advocacy

To provide a network of support and a voice for Liberian nationals in Gisenyi.
